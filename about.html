About Us

Pure Honey from Kenya's Heartland

Baringo Gold Honey is sourced from the pristine landscapes of Baringo, Kenya, where wildflowers and acacia trees provide the perfect nectar for our bees.

Our honey is harvested using traditional methods that ensure purity and unmatched taste, straight from the hive to your table. We work directly with local beekeepers to support sustainable practices and fair trade.
